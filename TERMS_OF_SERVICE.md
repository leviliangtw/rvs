## 📋 1. The Service

RVS is a free, open-source browser extension that synchronizes video playback — play, pause, seek, and playback rate — between **two remote users** watching the same video on supported sites (currently YouTube and Netflix). To coordinate playback, a lightweight signaling server relays sync messages between the two peers in a shared room.

The Service exists for one narrow purpose: **real-time playback synchronization of web videos between remote users.** It does not download media, record content, block ads, or track usage. See the [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md) for details on what is transmitted.
